My Top 3 Movies

The Mummy (1999)

The Mummy is an action-adventure film that combines elements of horror and fantasy. Directed by Stephen Sommers, it stars Brendan Fraser as Rick O'Connell, a soldier of fortune who teams up with a librarian (Rachel Weisz) and her brother (John Hannah) to battle an ancient Egyptian priest who has been resurrected. The film is known for its thrilling action sequences, special effects, and a blend of humor and suspense that keeps viewers engaged from start to finish.

George of The Jungle

George of the Jungle is a comedy film that parodies the Tarzan story.

Avatar

Avatar is a science fiction. It is set in th e mid-22nd century on the lush alien world of Pandora, where humans are mining a valuable mineral called unobtanium.
